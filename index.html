About FDKZN
Our mission and values

Football Deaf KwaZulu-Natal (FDKZN) is a community football league dedicated to providing competitive and inclusive football opportunities for deaf and hard-of-hearing players across the province.

Founded to bridge the gap between mainstream football and the deaf community, FDKZN organises regular fixtures, tournaments, and development programmes for players of all skill levels across the province.

We work closely with the South African Deaf Sports Federation and local football clubs to grow the beautiful game within KwaZulu-Natal's deaf community.

Inclusion
Football for every deaf and hard-of-hearing player in KZN, regardless of background or ability.
Community
Building lasting bonds between players, families, and supporters across the province.
Excellence
Raising the standard of deaf football through quality coaching and competitive fixtures.
Respect
Promoting sportsmanship, fair play, and dignity on and off the pitch.
